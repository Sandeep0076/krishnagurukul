Welcome to Krishna Gurukul School

At Krishna Gurukul School, we are dedicated to providing an enriching and supportive educational experience for our students. We are excited to welcome new students and families into our school community.

Our admission process is designed to ensure that students are a good fit for our educational environment, and that our school is the right place for their growth and development.

Admission Process

Simple steps to secure your child's future with us

Step 1: Application

Submit the admission application form with all required details and information about your child.

Step 2: Documents

Submit all required documents including birth certificate, academic transcripts, and photographs.

Step 3: Interview

Attend a brief interaction session with our admission committee to understand your child's needs.

Step 4: Confirmation

Pay the admission fee and complete the enrollment process to secure your child's admission.

Required Documents

Please ensure you have the following documents ready for submission

Essential Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original + Copy)
  • Academic Transcripts (Previous School)
  • Aadhar Card (Student + Parents)
  • Passport-sized Photographs (4 copies)

Additional Requirements

  • Medical Certificate
  • Address Proof
  • Character Certificate (If applicable)
  • Fee Payment Receipt

Important Note

All documents should be submitted in original along with photocopies. Original documents will be returned after verification.
