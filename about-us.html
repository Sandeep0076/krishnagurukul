About Krishna Gurukul School

Discover our commitment to excellence in education and character development

Who We Are

We are an English-Hindi Medium Co-Educational school recognized by the Punjab School Education Board. The school year begins in April and ends in March of the following year.

The academic grades at our school range from Pre-Nursery to Grade 10th. The course of study is arranged according to the syllabus laid down by P.S.E.B and also in accordance with the state government guidelines.

At Krishna Gurukul School, we believe in providing a nurturing environment where students can develop both academically and personally, preparing them to become responsible global citizens.

Our Mission

At Krishna Gurukul School, we prepare children for the future by providing knowledge and skills to achieve academic excellence, personal growth, and success in a safe, positive and diverse environment.

We believe in a student-centric philosophy, wherein we respect the individual differences and find success in each student in one way or the other. Students are valued for their individuality and diverse capabilities as they are taught to face the future and contribute to society with compassion, courage, knowledge and vision.

Our Core Focus

  • Academic Excellence
  • Character Development
  • Individual Growth
  • Global Citizenship

Our Vision

At Krishna Gurukul School, our vision is to develop well-rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential and enabling each student to flourish as a responsible citizen in the global community.

Message from the Managing Director
