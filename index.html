Welcome to the Leadership Corner At KRISHNA GURUKUL SCHOOL, we believe that strong leadership is the foundation of personal growth and community success. We are committed to nurturing the leaders of tomorrow and providing students with opportunities to develop their leadership skills and qualities.

Core Values

A core value is a central belief clearly understood and shared by every member of the school community. we beleive in commitment, opportunity respect and excellence.

By commitment we mean that everyone

  • Shows loyalty to friends colleagues and the school
  • Is willing to support and show care for those who need help
  • Keeps the school rules.

By opportunity we mean that everyone

  • Develops confidence through participation
  • Considers their own strenghs
  • Sets personal goals for improvement.

By respect we mean that everyone

  • Values all members of the school community.
  • Displays tolerance of others with different points of view and beliefs.
  • Shows respect for the school buildings, facilities and surronding Environment

By excellence we mean that everyone

  • Is proud of personal achievement
  • Produces work of the highest quality
  • Makes best use of talents, time and resourses

#Aims of the School

Krishana Gurukul school, in keeping with its motto esperance, aspires to develop a sense of belonging and pride in the school through :-

  1. Commitment by all members of the school community to its caring etros and academic traditions.
  2. opportunity within a supportive Environment for all to fulfill their potential as individuals, lifelong learns and contributers to society.
  3. Respect for others and self, and the building of harmonoius relationship within the school and the wider community
  4. Excellence, personal achievement and high standards in all areas of school life.
